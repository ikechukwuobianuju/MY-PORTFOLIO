Power BI Projects

Student Mental Health analysis

Background: This extensive dataset offers crucial insights into the mental health challenges students face and seeks to guide strategies for enhancing mental health support and interventions in educational settings. Dataset was provided by Quantum analytics NG. The dataset was cleaned on power query and the dashboard created on power BI.

Goal: The primary goal of the student mental health analysis is to understand and identify the key factors contributing to mental health issues, thereby influencing the mental well-being of students.

Key insights: Females are more likely to experience depression and anxiety compared to males. Students pursuing BCS and Engineering courses are more prone to panic attacks than those in other fields. Additionally, individuals aged 18-19 years old are more likely to develop depression compared to older age groups.

Recommendation: Increase the availability of mental health professionals in schools, Provide adequate and regular mental health screenings and assessments and ensure easy access to counseling services and support groups.

Maternal Health Risk Analysis

Background: The Maternal Health Risk dataset is a detailed compilation of data aimed at highlighting the factors affecting maternal health outcomes. It is crucial for understanding and addressing the complexities of maternal health, particularly in reducing maternal morbidity and mortality. Dataset was provided by quantum analytics NG. It was collected and transformed on power query editor before visualizing on power BI.

The goal of this analysis is to gain insights into factors contributing to maternal health risks and to identify patterns and trends that can inform healthcare strategies

Key Insight: Adults and seniors have high chances of suffering High Blood sugar level, SistolicBp and DiastolicBp than teens and youth, which could be attibuted to age factor.

Recommendation: Implement regular and thorough health screenings for pregnant women, focusing on BP, BS, and heart rate and also ensure pregnant women with high BP and BS have easy access to specialists such as endocrinologists and cardiologists.

Retail Sales Analysis

Retail data analysis entails scrutinizing data from retail operations to gain business insights. This process aids retailers in making informed decisions, optimizing operations, improving customer experience, and boosting sales growth.

Goal: Identify sales trends and opportunities to boost revenue, also to tailor shopping experiences according to individual customer preferences and purchasing patterns.

key insight: More visitors, particularly women, prefer Mall of Istanbul and Kanyon Mall over other malls in Turkey. There has been a significant decline in sales from 2021 to 2023, with females contributing more revenue than males.

Recommendation:Implement initiatives such as loyalty programs or exclusive events to attract and retain customers to other malls with least patronage. Develop targeted marketing campaigns that appeal to both male and female shoppers to boast more sales and revenue.

Supply Chain Optimization Analysis for FMCG Company

Supply chain optimization analysis for a Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) company involves evaluating and improving the processes related to the production, distribution, and delivery of products.

Goal: is to identify areas that affect the operation of FMCG Company that can lead to increase efficiency, lower costs, and ensure products are delivered on time to meet consumer demand.

Key Insighs: People in rural areas face more transportation challenges, leading to potential product shortages. Warehouses in the North and West experience greater transportation issues compared to other regions, which can impact the distribution of goods to retailers and target markets. Additionally, competition is higher in the North and West than in other area.

Recommendation: Streamline supply chain processes to guarantee timely product delivery, boosting customer satisfaction and loyalty. Additionally, optimize inventory management to ensure products are readily available where and when customers need them.
