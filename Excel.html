HR Dataset Analysis

This project entails anlyzing HR datasets by investigating different elements of employee data, including demographics, performance metrics, turnover rates, recruitment efficiency, and additional factors. The dataset was provided by Quantum analytics NG, it was cleaned on power query before Visulizing on Excel.

Goal:The goal is to identify trends and patterns in employee behavior, performance metrics, turnover rate, recruitment effectiveness and more

Key Insight: Total 87 employees were employeed through indeed and LinkedIn when compared with other recruitment sources. More employee are centered in production technician than other department. 66.58% are still active employee, 28.30% resigned voluntrily while 5.14% employment were termited for a reason

Recommendation: Collaborate with IT, Finance, Sales and Operations to collect relevant data and gain diverse perspectives on HR issues. Ensure HR data analysis is continuous by regularly updating datasets and monitoring key metrics to adapt strategies over time.

Sales Dataset Analysis

This project is a visualization of sales transaction seeking to comprehend their sales performance, refine their strategies, and promote growth. Dataset was provided by Quantum analytics NG. It was cleaned and transformed on power query editor before vizualizing on Excel.

Goal: The goal of sales dataset analysis is to understand sales performance, spot trends, optimize strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and boost revenue growth. This process involves analyzing customer behavior, assessing product performance, refining sales processes, and making informed decisions to achieve business goals

Key Insight: Some region generates more sales than other region.The trend analysis shows that there was decrease in revenue and price of items in the last quater of the year.

Recommendation: Analyze customer behavior and preferences to identify opportunities for upselling, cross-selling, and personalized marketing. To harness other regions for possible sales and generate more revenue.
