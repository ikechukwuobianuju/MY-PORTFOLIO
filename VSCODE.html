Maternal Health Risk Analysis

Background: The Maternal Health Risk dataset is a detailed compilation of data aimed at highlighting the factors affecting maternal health outcomes. It is crucial for understanding and addressing the complexities of maternal health, particularly in reducing maternal morbidity and mortality.Dataset was provided by quantum analytics NG. It was collected and transformed on power query editor before visualizing on power BI.

The goal Of this analysis is to gain insights into factors contributing to maternal health risks and to identify patterns and trends that can inform healthcare strategies

Key Insight: Adults and seniors have high chances of suffering High Blood sugar level, SistolicBp and DiastolicBp than teens and youth,which could be attibuted to age factor.

Recommendation: Implement regular and thorough health screenings for pregnant women, focusing on BP, BS, and heart rate and also ensure pregnant women with high BP and BS have easy access to specialists such as endocrinologists and cardiologists.

Retail Sales Analysis

Retail data analysis entails scrutinizing data from retail operations to gain business insights. This process aids retailers in making informed decisions, optimizing operations, improving customer experience, and boosting sales growth.

Goal: Identify sales trends and opportunities to boost revenue, also to tailor shopping experiences according to individual customer preferences and purchasing patterns.

key insight: More visitors, particularly women, prefer Mall of Istanbul and Kanyon Mall over other malls in Turkey. There has been a significant decline in sales from 2021 to 2023, with females contributing more revenue than males

Recommendation:Implement initiatives such as loyalty programs or exclusive events to attract and retain customers to other malls with least patronage. Develop targeted marketing campaigns that appeal to both male and female shoppers to boast more sales and revenue
